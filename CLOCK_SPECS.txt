===============================================================
PHYSICAL SIZE ESTIMATES
===============================================================
All numbers are approximate from vendor datasheets; enclosure
totals assume a basic 3D-printed shell with 3-5 mm wall thickness
and standoffs for the Pi + HAT stack.


--- Displays (panels alone) ---

Waveshare 5" 1080×1080 round LCD (HDMI)
  Active area:   ~127 mm diameter (circular)
  PCB outline:   ~138 × 138 mm (square board, round visible area)
  Thickness:     ~12–15 mm (incl. driver board)

Waveshare 3.4" 800×800 round DSI (Goodix touch)
  Active area:   ~86 mm diameter (circular)
  PCB outline:   ~95 × 95 mm
  Thickness:     ~10 mm

Waveshare 5" 800×480 DSI (B), capacitive
  Active area:   ~120.7 × 75.6 mm
  Outline:       ~150.7 × 95.45 mm
  Thickness:     ~10–12 mm


--- SBCs ---

Raspberry Pi 5         85 × 56 × 17 mm  (≈ 46 g)
Raspberry Pi 4B        85 × 56 × 17 mm  (≈ 46 g)
Raspberry Pi Zero 2 W  65 × 30 × 5 mm   (≈ 11 g)


--- HATs / Power ---

SunFounder Fusion HAT     ~65 × 56 mm board, +~10 mm stack height
  + 18650 cell holder      adds ~25 mm depth with battery installed
Waveshare UPS HAT          ~65 × 56 mm board, +~12 mm with cells



===============================================================
ENCLOSURE SIZE ESTIMATES (stacked + cased)
===============================================================
Conservative estimates for a finished, wall-or-shelf-mountable
unit. "D" = deepest dimension when display faces the viewer.


--- SuperClockFast (round, battery, Pi 5 + Fusion HAT) ---
Bezel/face:    ~150 × 150 mm  (round face cut from square front)
Total depth:   ~55–65 mm
                (display 14 + Pi 5 17 + HAT 10 + battery 25 + walls 5–10)
Weight est:    ~280–350 g w/ 18650, no mounting hardware

--- SmallSuperClock (round, 3.4", Pi 4B, no battery) ---
Bezel/face:    ~105 × 105 mm
Total depth:   ~35–45 mm
                (display 10 + Pi 4B 17 + walls 8)
Weight est:    ~180–220 g

--- superclok-square (rectangular, Pi 4B + UPS HAT) ---
Bezel/face:    ~160 × 105 mm
Total depth:   ~45–55 mm
                (display 12 + Pi 4B 17 + UPS HAT 12 + walls 6–10)
Weight est:    ~250–300 g w/ cells

--- SuperClock-Slow (round, 5" 1080×1080, Pi Zero 2 W, wall) ---
Bezel/face:    ~150 × 150 mm
Total depth:   ~30–40 mm  (slimmest of the fleet — Zero is only 5 mm)
                (display 14 + Zero 5 + walls 8 + HDMI/USB adapters 5–10)
Weight est:    ~180–220 g
Notes:         A bulky HDMI-to-micro-HDMI adapter or a flexible
               ribbon will determine how flat the back can be.
